Help! How do I stay safe during a hurricane?

Know

- The category and the risks (risk increases if it is a category 3+)
- How to stay informed
- The evacuation and transportation plan (if needed)

Store

- People: plan where people and pets would go during evacuation
- Prescriptions: medicines, medical equipment, glasses, hearing aids
- Papers: important documents (hard/soft copies)
- Personal Needs: e.g. clothes, food, water, cash, phones, chargers etc.
- Priceless Items: e.g. irreplacable photos, mementos, other valuables
- Power: have items to survive without power

- By buying specialized insurance for losses caused by flood or wind damage

Discuss

- What you have done to prepare to family, friends, colleagues, etc.
- How your community can reduce risk
